Section 2.3 Indexed Collections

Worksheet 2.3.1 More Indexed Collection Exercises

1.

For an index set \(\mathbb{Z}\) with \(n\in\mathbb{Z},\text{,}\) we define the family of sets

\begin{equation*} S_n = \bigl\{ n, n+1, n+2, n+3 \bigr\}. \end{equation*}

  1. Describe the sets \(S_1,S_5,\) and \(S_{-2}\text{.}\)

  2. Describe \(S_1\cup S_3\text{.}\)

  3. Describe \(\bigcap_{i=1}^3 S_i\) .

  4. Describe \(\bigcup_{n\in\mathbb{Z}} S_n\) .

  5. Describe \(\bigcap_{n\in\mathbb{Z}} S_n\) .

Solution

2.

For an index set \(\mathbb{N}\) with \(n\in\mathbb{N},\text{,}\) we define the family of sets

\begin{equation*} A_n = (-n,n). \end{equation*}

  1. Describe the sets \(A_1,A_5,\) and \(A_{10}\text{.}\)

  2. Describe \(A_1\cup A_2\text{.}\)

  3. Describe \(\bigcap_{i=1}^3 A_i\) .

  4. Describe \(\bigcup_{n\in\mathbb{N}} A_n\) .

  5. Describe \(\bigcap_{n\in\mathbb{N}} A_n\) .

Solution

3.

\begin{equation*} B_n = \left[ 0,\frac{1}{n} \right). \end{equation*}

  2. Describe \(B_1\cap B_2\text{.}\)

  4. Describe \(\bigcup_{n\in\mathbb{N}} B_n\) .

  5. Describe \(\bigcap_{n\in\mathbb{N}} B_n\) .
