Remember there are two implications to prove:

  1. If \(5\mid x\) then \(5\mid x^2\text{.}\)

  2. If \(5\mid x^2\) then \(5\mid x\text{.}\)

Try not to jump directly toward an indirect proof -- always at least consider the direct approach in each case. “What would the direct assumption give me to work with? Is it enough to proceed?”
